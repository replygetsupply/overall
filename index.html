Documentation

Everything you need to know about creating tokens on Capsule

What is Capsule?

Capsule is a platform that allows you to create and launch tokens on the Solana blockchain with built-in fee sharing capabilities. Whether you're launching a community token, a creator coin, or a project token, Capsule makes it simple and accessible.

Token Creation

Required Information

  • Token Name: The full name of your token
  • Ticker: A short symbol (e.g., BTC, ETH)
  • Description: What your token represents
  • Image: A visual representation of your token

Optional Features

  • Social Links: Twitter, Telegram, Website
  • Fee Sharing: Distribute fees to multiple wallets
  • Initial Buy: Purchase tokens at launch

Fee Sharing

One of Capsule's unique features is the ability to share trading fees with up to 100 different wallet addresses. This is perfect for:

  • Rewarding team members and contributors
  • Distributing revenue to stakeholders
  • Creating incentive structures for your community
  • Building sustainable tokenomics

Initial Buy

When launching your token, you have the option to make an initial purchase using SOL. This helps:

  • Establish initial liquidity for your token
  • Protect against snipers and bots
  • Show commitment to your project
  • Set a floor price for your token

We recommend a minimum of 0.5 SOL for optimal protection.

Technical Details

Blockchain: Solana

Token Standard: SPL Token

Fee Distribution: Automatic via smart contract

Maximum Fee Recipients: 100 wallets

Fee Distribution

When you enable fee sharing, trading fees are automatically distributed according to your specified allocation:

  • Real-time distribution on every trade
  • No manual claims required
  • Transparent on-chain tracking
  • Split among up to 100 recipients
