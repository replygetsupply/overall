01 — About

The Vision Toly Had

For years, Toly has called for a "DevWheel" — a sustainable way to bring fresh talent into the Solana ecosystem. The idea was simple: create a self-perpetuating cycle that continuously onboards developers who want to build.

We're tired of redeploy bundles and quick-flip schemes. Why not actually help? The Developer FlyWheel commits 100% of creator fees to onboarding devs from platforms like GitHub Sponsors and Kickstarter. Real developers. Real funding. Real impact.

02 — How

The Rotation System

The mechanism is straightforward and transparent. Every time our creator fees accumulate to $100, we send the full amount to one developer in our rotation queue.

We cycle through each developer until they've received their complete funding goal. Then we move to the next. No complicated tokenomics. No hidden fees. Just a direct pipeline from community activity to developer support. The flywheel keeps spinning.

03 — Why

The Trenches Need Change

The crypto space has been dominated by short-term thinking for too long. Pump and dumps. Rug pulls. Hollow promises. The trenches are exhausted.

We believe the future is built by developers, not by speculators. By redirecting value back to builders, we create something that actually matters — a growing ecosystem of talented people who are here to create, not extract. This is how we change things.
